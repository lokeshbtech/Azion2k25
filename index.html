Box Cricket :

Get ready for an action-packed Box Cricket tournament at our symposium! This fast-paced format features 5-member teams competing in a 5-over showdown. With unique rules like one-pitch, one-hand catches, no sixes, and a restriction to only medium or slow bowling, the game tests precision, teamwork, and strategy. Gather your squad, play smart, and claim victory in the box!

Event Details :

RULE 1: Each team must consist of 5 members.

RULE 2:The match will be 5 overs per side. Any one player from a team can bowl 2 overs, while the others can bowl only 1 over each.

RULE 3: One-pitch, one-hand catch is considered out.

RULE 4: Players are allowed to hit only fours; sixes are not allowed.

RULE 5: The umpire’s decision is final—players must not argue with the umpire.

RULE 6: Bat and ball will be provided by the organizers.
